# 🩺 Welcome to RemedyRadar

> *You describe how you feel. We find who can help.*

**RemedyRadar** is an agentic health specialist finder that maps your symptoms to the right medical specialist near you — with transparent reasoning for every recommendation.

### How to use:
1. **Describe your symptoms** in plain language
2. **Include your city** (e.g., "I'm in Bangalore" or "near San Francisco")
3. Optionally add **preferences** (e.g., "prefer a female doctor", "under ₹1000")

### Try these examples:
- *"I've been having persistent chest tightness when I exercise, I'm in Bangalore"*
- *"Recurring headaches with visual aura for the past 3 months, San Francisco"*
- *"My child has had a fever and rash for 2 days, we're in Bangalore"*
- *"I have chronic lower back pain that radiates down my left leg"*

### ⚠️ Important
This tool **does not diagnose** medical conditions. It helps you find the right specialist to consult. In case of a medical emergency, call **911** (US) or **112** (India) immediately.
